## How to Get Started in 5 Steps

### 1. Prepare Your CSV Data
Create a spreadsheet with headers like `Name`, `Phone`, `Email`, and `Company`. Save this document as a **.csv** file.

### 2. Upload Your File
Think of this file as the "fuel" for your outreach campaign. Simply upload it to the dashboard to begin.

### 3. Craft Your Template
Use our **Smart Tags** to personalize your messages. Instead of typing "Hello Customer," use "Hello @Name." The system will automatically swap the tag for the actual name from your file.

### 4. Generate Links
Tap **Generate Link** for WhatsApp messages or **Generate Draft** for Email campaigns.

### 5. Send to Your Leads
Click **Send** to launch the message directly through your native apps. No manual typing required!
