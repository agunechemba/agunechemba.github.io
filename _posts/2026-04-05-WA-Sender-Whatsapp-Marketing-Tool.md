## How to Get Started in 3 Steps

### 1. Prepare Your CSV Data
Create a spreadsheet with headers like `Name`, `Phone`, `Email`, and `Company`. This is the "fuel" for your outreach.

### 2. Craft Your Template
Use our **Smart Tags**. Instead of typing "Hello Customer," type "Hello @name." The system automatically swaps the tag for the actual name in your file.

### 3. Generate and Send
Click **Generate**, and the tool creates a personalized link for every contact. Tap **Generate Link** (WhatsApp) or **Generate Draft** (Email) to launch the message directly through your native apps.
