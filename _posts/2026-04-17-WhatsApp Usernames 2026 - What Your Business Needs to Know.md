 # WhatsApp Usernames 2026: What Your Business Needs to Know
 
<img src="https://i.ibb.co/FbTV4kCD/Whatsapp-username.png" width="100%">

Big news is coming to WhatsApp later this year, and if you’ve been looking for a more professional way to connect with your customers, this update is specifically for you.

WhatsApp is officially introducing **usernames**, a feature designed to prioritize privacy while giving your brand a much stronger identity. 

Soon, you’ll be able to move beyond just sharing a phone number and instead use a unique handle like **@yourbusiness** to make it easier for new leads to find and message you directly.

For those of you who value privacy, this is a major win. When new customers reach out, they can choose to display their username rather than their personal phone number.

This lowers the barrier for people who might be hesitant to share their contact details right away, making your "Click to WhatsApp" strategies more effective than ever.

While your current chats and saved contacts won’t change at all, your business systems will need to be ready to support these new identifiers alongside traditional phone numbers.

As we head toward June 2026, now is the time to audit your CRM and internal workflows. You should be prepared to request phone numbers through WhatsApp’s upcoming built-in tools whenever they are strictly necessary for shipping or payments.

Most importantly, keep a close watch for the upcoming window to **reserve your business username**.

Securing your brand handle early ensures you stay discoverable and professional as the platform evolves.

It’s a simple shift, but it’s one that will make your business feel more accessible and secure for every new lead that comes your way.
